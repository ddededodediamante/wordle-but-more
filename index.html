Color Meanings

  • Correct: Exact match (right letter & spot)
  • Mirror: Right letter, mirrored position
  • Present: Letter exists, wrong spot
  • Alphabet: A nearby letter in the alphabet is in the word (e.g. "B" → "A" or "C")
  • Keyboardic: A nearby key is in the word (e.g. "Q" → "W" or "A")
  • Phonetic: Same type (vowel/consonant) in the same spot
  • ddededodediamante: Letter from "ddededodediamante"
  • Absent: Letter isn't in the word at all
